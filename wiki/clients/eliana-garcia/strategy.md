## Positioning

Eliana should not be positioned as a generic lawyer. The first campaign should position her around concrete legal problems that the buyer recognizes immediately and can send by WhatsApp.

The operating promise is not "we solve your case". The promise is a first legal review / next step from the right artifact:

- accident photos and insurance details;
- medical certificate or ART documentation;
- family-law problem and monthly process organization.

## Priority Cases

1. **Accidentes de transito**
   - Why: high urgency, clear trigger, easy buyer recognition on Instagram/Facebook.
   - Best-fit cases: Mendoza accidents with good insurance behind the other party, especially material damage plus physical damage.
   - Intake artifact: photo of the vehicle, accident details, insurance information, medical certificate if there is injury.
   - Buyer-facing hook: `Te chocaron en Mendoza?`
   - CTA: send the photo/documentation by WhatsApp.

2. **ART / accidentes laborales**
   - Why: Eliana said ART can be strong and fast when the damage is large.
   - Best-fit cases: fractures, obvious workplace injury, physical damage that is hard for ART to deny.
   - Avoid leading with low-value/ambiguous injuries like cervicalgia or hernia unless later data proves they convert profitably.
   - Intake artifact: medical certificate, ART rejection/notice, work accident details.
   - Buyer-facing hook: `Accidente laboral?`
   - CTA: send the certificate by WhatsApp.

3. **Familia**
   - Why: Eliana likes the area and uses it as recurring monthly revenue.
   - Best-fit cases: people with stable income who can pay a monthly fee while the process moves.
   - Buyer-facing problems: cuota alimentaria, regimen de comunicacion/visitas, divorcio, agreements that need organization.
   - Intake artifact: current agreement, court document, short explanation of the conflict.
   - Buyer-facing hook: `Cuota, visitas o divorcio?`
   - CTA: consult by WhatsApp.

## Geography

Start in **Mendoza**. Eliana does not yet have a fixed Buenos Aires partner. Buenos Aires can be tested later only if the partner economics are clear, because going 50/50 with another study lowers margin while Eliana still carries acquisition cost.
