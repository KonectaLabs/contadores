# Konecta Frankie Video Offer

For Meta ad image generation, read `konecta-meta-ads` before drafting prompts.
It is the required source for problem-first visuals and compact trust/action
cues.

## Core Rule

Make the offer do the selling.

Do not lead with salesmanship, technology, agency services, calls, or "more leads" in general. Lead with a specific, tangible, valuable outcome the buyer already wants.

Bad:
- "Te hacemos una web."
- "Corremos Meta Ads."
- "Te traemos mas leads."
- "Implementamos IA para tu estudio."

Good:
- "Mas consultas de sucesiones en tu WhatsApp, con herederos, inmueble y documentacion inicial."
- "Consultas por despidos filtradas antes de que el abogado pierda tiempo respondiendo de cero."
- "Amparos con urgencia y datos clave listos para revision profesional."

## Offer Formula

Build every funnel offer in this order:

1. Choose one buyer.
   - Use a narrow buyer with a painful, valuable problem.
   - Avoid selling to the entire industry at once.

2. Choose one economically attractive case or opportunity.
   - Ask what cases the buyer actually wants.
   - Prefer specific cases that are fast, profitable, repeatable, or high-value.

3. Promise a tangible outcome, not a service.
   - Say what arrives, changes, or gets delivered.
   - Do not say only the mechanism.

4. Demonstrate one real-looking example.
   - Show a WhatsApp, form fill, CRM card, document checklist, booking, or lead record.
   - Use a synthetic example when needed, but make it look like the actual artifact the buyer would receive.
   - Avoid stock photos, vague dashboards, smiley handshakes, robots, circuits, or generic AI visuals.

5. Present price as value received.
   - Do not just state the price.
   - Use the frame: "Y por eso recibis..."
   - Compare price against the value of the specific opportunity when it is ethical and defensible.

## Video Pitch Arc

Use this arc for Loom videos and 60-second decks:

1. Outcome first.
   - Open on the desired artifact.
   - Example: "Mira esta consulta de sucesion entrando ya ordenada al WhatsApp del estudio."

2. Name the pain.
   - Say the everyday frustration in the buyer's language.
   - Example: "Hoy muchas consultas llegan por audios, datos incompletos y mensajes que despues se enfrian."

3. Show the demonstration.
   - Make one example visual do the heavy lifting.
   - Show before/after or show the finished lead/ficha directly.

4. Explain the mechanism after the outcome is clear.
   - Landing, WhatsApp, preguntas, documentacion, resumen, CRM, seguimiento.
   - Keep the mechanism short.

5. Present the investment with "and for that you get".
   - If price is unknown, leave a clear placeholder and ask Facundo for setup/monthly terms.

6. Close with one next step.
   - Calendly, WhatsApp reply, or "te muestro como se veria para tu area".

## Ad Rules

Generate ads from the buyer's desired case, not from the agency service.

For direct-response ad images, do not lead with the professional's name, logo,
initials, or personal brand. The buyer should first see their own problem and
the outcome they want. Use the provider identity only if the user explicitly
asks for a brand-awareness creative or a required disclosure.
After the problem and outcome are visually clear, a compact trust/action cue is
allowed: `Abogada`, a small legal/balance icon, or a WhatsApp-style contact
signal. It should help the buyer understand who can help and how to act without
turning the creative into a brand ad.

For each ad, specify:

- the case type;
- the exact opportunity that arrives;
- the visual artifact;
- the on-image text;
- why this angle tests something different.

Keep on-image copy direct and mobile-readable. Prefer:

- "Mas consultas de sucesiones para tu estudio"
- "Mas casos de despidos en tu WhatsApp"
- "Recibi consultas de amparos mejor calificadas"

Avoid:

- "Marketing para abogados"
- "IA para estudios juridicos"
- "Mas leads"
- "Soy [nombre]"
- "[Nombre] Abogada"
- footer/logo-first layouts
- removing all legal/contact context when a small trust cue would improve response
- "Clientes garantizados"
- "Ganamos tu juicio"
- "Plata facil"

## Demonstration Examples

Use one concrete example per asset. If a niche has its own trained skill, use that niche's examples instead of inventing new ones.

Good demonstration artifacts:

- WhatsApp inquiry;
- form fill;
- CRM card;
- document checklist;
- booking record;
- before/after lead record.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise legal outcomes.
- Do not claim clients, cases, revenue, rankings, or appointments are guaranteed.
- Do not make the AI look like it gives legal advice.
- Label outputs as preliminary for professional review.
- Use fictitious data in demos.
- Keep ads sober enough for professional services.
