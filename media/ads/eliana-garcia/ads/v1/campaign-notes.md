# Eliana Garcia - Ads v1

Source:
- Call transcript: `wiki/clients/eliana-garcia/2026-05-19-meeting-transcript.md`

Campaign thesis:
- Start in Mendoza with three concrete case paths: traffic accidents, ART/work accidents, and family law.
- Each ad should make the buyer recognize the problem and know what to send by WhatsApp.
- Do not lead with generic lawyer branding, biography, or "marketing para abogados".

## Pieces

1. `01-accidente-transito-mendoza.png`
   - Angle: traffic accident in Mendoza.
   - Trigger: the buyer was hit and has car damage / insurance details.
   - CTA: send the photo by WhatsApp.
   - On-image text: `TE CHOCARON EN MENDOZA?`, `Reclamo a aseguradora`, `Mandanos la foto por WhatsApp`.
   - Why it tests something different: urgent consumer-side accident intent with a clear document/photo artifact.

2. `02-accidente-laboral-art.png`
   - Angle: workplace accident / ART.
   - Trigger: the buyer has an injury and a medical certificate.
   - CTA: send the certificate by WhatsApp.
   - On-image text: `ACCIDENTE LABORAL?`, `Reclamo ART en Mendoza`, `Manda el certificado por WhatsApp`.
   - Why it tests something different: high-value injury cases with an obvious next artifact, without promising a payout.

3. `03-familia-proceso-mensual.png`
   - Angle: family law process.
   - Trigger: cuota, visitas, or divorce need order and follow-through.
   - CTA: consult by WhatsApp.
   - On-image text: `CUOTA, VISITAS O DIVORCIO?`, `Proceso ordenado mes a mes`, `Consultanos por WhatsApp`.
   - Why it tests something different: recurring monthly service fit for family cases, without making a custody/alimony guarantee.

## Guardrails

- No guarantee of legal result, payout, custody outcome, or insurance settlement.
- No official court, government, insurance, ART, Facebook, or Instagram logos.
- The v1 previews use WhatsApp-style messaging signals; Alan should confirm whether to upload as-is or ask for brand-safe generic chat-icon variants before final campaign upload.
- Keep Mendoza as the first geography.
- Mini-pymes and Buenos Aires expansion are later tests, not v1.
