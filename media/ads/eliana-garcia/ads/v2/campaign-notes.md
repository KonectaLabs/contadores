# Eliana Garcia - Ads v2

Correction applied:
- Removed provider-first branding from the image: no lawyer name, no logo, no initials, no brand footer.
- Made each creative more visual and direct-response oriented.
- First read is the buyer's problem; second read is the desired solution.

## Pieces

1. `01-te-chocaron-aseguradora.png`
   - Problem visual: damaged car and stressed driver.
   - On-image text: `TE CHOCARON?`, `Reclama a la aseguradora`, `Te ayudamos a cobrar`.
   - Intent: someone who just had a car accident should recognize the situation immediately.

2. `02-art-no-te-pago.png`
   - Problem visual: injured worker with arm in sling, hard hat, and medical certificate.
   - On-image text: `ACCIDENTE LABORAL?`, `La ART no te pago?`, `Te ayudamos a reclamar`.
   - Intent: workplace-injury cases where the client has not been paid by ART.

3. `03-divorcio-cuota-proceso.png`
   - Problem visual: separated couple, divorce papers, visits calendar, and child-support expenses.
   - On-image text: `DIVORCIO O CUOTA?`, `No lo dejes avanzar solo`, `Ordena el proceso`.
   - Intent: family-law cases where the buyer wants order and a next step.

## Rule Captured

For this style of ad, do not waste first-screen space on who the professional is.
The creative must communicate:

- `se cual es tu problema`;
- `te ayudo a conseguir la solucion que queres`.
